Be a Voice

Learn how you can help volunteer for Voice of the Marytrs ministry.

BE THEIR VOICE.
STAND WITH THEM.

We support Christians who are or have been persecuted for their faith and involvement in spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ in a hostile environment. We give their testimony a voice, informing and mobilising Christians in Australia to stand with their persecuted brothers and sisters in Christ.

Becoming a Voice for the Voice of the Martyrs requires a commitment to serving the persecuted church and spreading awareness about their persecution. You can learn more about volunteer opportunities by contacting Voice of the Martyrs ministry directly through their contact page or by emailing them at info@vom.com.au to inquire about becoming a voice.
