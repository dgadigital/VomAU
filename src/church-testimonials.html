New Heart Baptist

“Jesse and Voice of the Martyrs are outstanding! Their presentation is informative and inviting. Their heart and passion is for Jesus Christ, His followers and God’s Kingdom; stirring and warming hearts with God’s love.”

New Heart Baptist
Brad Gardiner, Associate Pastor

Village Avenue Community Church

“Our church was blessed to have Jesse from VOM come and share stories of persecuted believers, the work of VOM and from God’s Word. It was a great reminder to pray for our brothers and sisters in chains around the world. We were deeply moved as a church body and what Jesse shared has continued to be a topic of conversation even weeks after his visit. We look forward to hearing from VOM in the future and will continue to make the persecuted church a matter of prayer and action.”

Village Avenue Community Church
Pastor Mike Deane

Tenthill Baptist Church

“As we live our comfortable Western lives with cars, air conditioning, peace, and comfort, it is easy to forget that not all Christians throughout the world experience the same thing. Voice of the Martyrs (VOM) reminds us to be praying for and supporting those throughout the world that are persecuted for the faith.”

Tenthill Baptist Church
Dale Buchanan, Senior Pastor

Oxley Uniting Church

“WOW – what an eye-opening encounter with Jesse from Voices of the Martyrs we enjoyed – not once, but twice – as he came and shared at both our Sunday services. We, in our relative comfy church settings, can learn a lot from those of our brothers and sisters who live daily under constant and highly volatile threats to their faith and indeed their life. Every church in the west needs to hear about the struggles, but also the lessons and challenges from the persecuted church. If you haven’t yet experienced the heart and passion of God’s agents in this field – you need to! I, and we, were certainly moved beyond words.”

Oxley Uniting Church
Reverend Dave Thomas

Beaudesert Baptist Church

“We were blessed to recently have Jesse share in our Sunday service. His presentation was informative, engaging and personally challenging. On a subsequent Sunday evening, we showed the ‘Sabina’ film. What a powerful story! I would strongly encourage you to consider having VOM share about those who suffer for the sake of Christ.”

Beaudesert Baptist Church
Pastor Josh Cocks

Agnes Water Baptist Church

“Jesse came and spoke to our church about the persecuted church in a way that really opened the eyes of our people to the need of Christ’s church worldwide. The impact of his talk and the ‘Sabina’ movie night we held is hard to overstate and will be felt for years to come. If your church is looking for someone to partner with to take seriously Christ’s call to love and identify with those who are suffering, I heartily recommend getting in touch with Voice of the Martyrs.”

Agnes Water Baptist Church
Dan Bassett, Lead Pastor
